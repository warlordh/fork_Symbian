Embedded stores

An embedded store can contain an arbitrarily complex network of streams and is a persistent store.

  • Introduction to embedded stores
    Data contained in a stream can represent a store in its own right rather than just objects. Such a store is known as an embedded store.
  • Constructing an embedded store
    Embedded stores are represented by an instance of the CEmbeddedStore class.
