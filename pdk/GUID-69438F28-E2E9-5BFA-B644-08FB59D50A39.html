SMS MTM Concepts

This section describes the Short Message Service (SMS) protocol and its implementation in Messaging Application module. SMS is a communications protocol to send and receive short text and numeric messages to and from mobile devices.

  • Types of SMS Message
  • SMS Settings
    The global settings that are applied to SMS MTM are known as the SMS service settings. Each SMS message entry has its own individual group of settings known as SMS message settings.
