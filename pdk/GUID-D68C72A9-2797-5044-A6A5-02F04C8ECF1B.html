Traffic Channel Tutorial

This tutorial describes how to set up a digital or analog traffic channel for a call in a CDMA network.

Calls set up during CDMA mode can request either a digital or analog traffic channel. The request may or may not be granted depending on network support.

  1. Use RMobileCall::SetTrafficChannel() to request a different traffic channel.
  2. Use RMobileCall::NotifyTrafficChannelConfirmation() to get the confirmation of a traffic channel request.
