Remote Connectivity Guide

Remote Connectivity enables a device to smoothly connect with a PC or other devices based on various transport bearers, such as USB, PTP-IP and WiFi. Contents on the device can be browsed, managed, synchronized and backed up.

  • Quick Start
    Remote Connectivity provides components and libraries that supports the connection, and data management and transfer between a device and a PC.
