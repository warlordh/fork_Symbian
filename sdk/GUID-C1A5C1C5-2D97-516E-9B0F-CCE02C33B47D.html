Playing Tones

This section introduces you to play a tone in different ways:

  • Playing Tones
    This document describes how to play various types of tones and how to pause and resume a tone.
  • Playing a Tone
    This tutorial introduces you to play a single tone.
  • Playing a DTMF Tone
    This tutorial describes about how to play a DTMF(Dual and Multi-Frequency) Tone.
  • Playing a Tone Sequence
    This tutorial describes how to play a sequence of tones.
  • Playing a Fixed Tone Sequence
    This tutorial introduces you to play in sequence a fixed tone.
  • Pausing and Resuming a Tone
    The CMMFDevSound::Pause() function pauses the play tone state. If the CMMFDevSound::Resume() function is supported, you can then use it to continue playing.
