List stretch

List stretching is commonly used in lists (that have two lines) viewed in landscape mode.

This also includes the settings list and lists with icons. By default, list stretching is ON. Applications can turn it OFF if needed.

Note: The list stretching can be turned ON or OFF using KAknAutomaticListStretching . The possible values of KAknAutomaticListStretching are 0 (list stretching OFF) and 1 (list stretching ON).
